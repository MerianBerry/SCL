bool scl::pack::Packager::write ( std::function< void(size_t, PackIndex *)>  cb = {})

Writes all submitted files to the pack. Invalidates all inactive pack files at the time of the call.

Warning
This function will call close(), and invalidate all info related to this packager.
Parameters
